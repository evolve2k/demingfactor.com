MyMoneyZen

Web Design, App Development

The MyMoneyZen Sisters (Kylea & Lauren) approached us to help them in their mission to create 'the most loved everyday money tool in the world'.

The brief was to design and develop a modern web application through which they could teach their innovative zen budgeting program.

We combined contemporary digital media design and agile software development principals to create an interactive online course that was simple to use and fun to work with.

The persona target of the app was Anne, a 25 year old single female who has recently moved out of home with a good friend and is paying the bills for the first time. Anne's organised in most areas of her life but has found it hard to grasp effectivley managing her day-to-day finances.
